Alexandre Vauthier Haute Couture Spring Summer 2017

Date: January 24, 2017

It was organised at Grand Palais, Paris. he collection presented a more approachable spin on couture, moving away from the ceremonial aspects of traditional haute couture. It featured a mix of high fashion and casual elements, such as denim shorts paired with voluminous taffeta tops and Swarovski crystal belts.The runway featured top models like Kendall Jenner and Bella Hadid

Marc Jacobs Fall/Winter 2020 fashion show

February 12, 2020 (as part of New York Fashion Week for the Fall/Winter 2020/2021 season)

This show had a more dramatic and theatrical vibe, with a focus on dark colors, luxurious fabrics, and intricate details.It was held at the Park Avenue Armory in New York City.

Some of the key locations included Skylight Clarkson Square, Industria, and a new venue on Fifth Avenue (608 Fifth Avenue)2. L'Oréal Paris "Le Défilé Walk Your Worth" fashion show in Paris during Paris Fashion Week in October 2023

Date:October 1st 2023

It was organised at the Esplanade of the Eiffel Tower in Paris2. This event marked the sixth edition of L'Oréal Paris' historic runway show, which aimed to celebrate women's empowerment, strength, and unity2.
